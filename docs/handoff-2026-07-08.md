## Product Direction

The best wedge is:

> Inventory, sourcing, and build capacity for Shopify sellers who depend on hard-to-source components.

For Umbrella Arcades / wall-mounted arcade cabinets, the system should track:

- Cabinet shells by color/finish/batch
- Displays
- Mini PCs / PC boards
- SSDs/storage
- Controllers
- Control boards/buttons/wiring
- Power/cabling/mounting hardware
- Packaging
- Labor/build/test steps
- Component substitutions
- Component failures/support tickets
- Vendor reliability over time

Supplier-side purchasing is core:

- Amazon
- eBay
- Alibaba

eBay needs special support:

- durable vendor master list
- per-listing offers
- best-offer/counteroffer history
- one-button ordering only after strict checks

## One-Button Ordering Rules

Keep automation conservative. One-button ordering should only be possible when:

- Item maps confidently to an internal component.
- Vendor is preferred or has enough positive history.
- Listing condition is allowed.
- Landed cost is below configured ceiling.
- Quantity is within limit.
- Listing has not changed since the last check.
- User sees a dry-run preview before final submit.

Negotiation should begin draft-only:

1. Detect eBay listings that accept offers.
2. Calculate suggested offer from margin, urgency, and price history.
3. Draft offer/message.
4. Require approval.
5. Record accepted/rejected/expired/countered outcomes.
